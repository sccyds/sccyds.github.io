Methodology.

Particularly, the OSEMN framework is adopted for all of my data science projects.
Why so, you may ask? Well, the OSEMN framework detailed a pragmatic approach for the entirety of a data science project.
Also, at every stage of the workflow, tuning process can be repeated until a satisfactory condition is met.

The OSEMN workflow is an acronym of the following:

  • O: Obtain data
  • S: Scrub/clean data
  • E: Explore/visualizing data to find patterns or trends
  • M: Modelling
  • N: iNterpret
